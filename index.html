The bug fixmanifesto

The purpose of this document is promoting good practices during the process of bug fixing. Have in mind that you may have different workflow and not all advices apply to your job.

The preparation

The bug, the problem, in your system should be document. It should be described somewhere. No matter if you are the developer that will work on it or the tester that found it. Create a ticket, issue, card, or whatever you use for managing your projects. Don't rely on your instant messenger for bug reporting. The email is a little bit better but still not the right place for such tasks.
